Sample Prompts

Here's a taste of what you're getting — just copy, paste, and customise the [brackets].

QUOTING
I run a CNC machining shop. I need to quote a job: - Part: [describe the part] - Material: [material type and grade] - Quantity: [number] - Operations: [list operations] - Tolerances: [general or specific] Help me estimate machine time, material cost, setup time, and a fair shop rate breakdown. Give me a range and flag anything I might be underestimating.
G-CODE TROUBLESHOOTING
I'm getting [describe problem] on my [machine + controller]. Here's the relevant G-code: [paste code block] What's likely wrong? Give me: 1. Most probable cause 2. How to fix it 3. What to check on the machine
NEW MATERIAL CHEAT SHEET
I've never machined [material] before. Give me: 1. Key properties that affect machining 2. Recommended tool types and coatings 3. Starting feeds and speeds 4. Common mistakes to avoid 5. Coolant recommendations 6. Safety considerations
